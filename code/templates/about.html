03 About
Drowsy driving ranks among the top causes of traffic accidents, significantly affecting road safety. Providing early warnings to sleepy drivers could prevent numerous accidents on the roads. That's why a team of students at Brown University decided to come together and utilize their knowledge of neural networks to combat the problem. Healey, Dave, Ivery, and Hadley, otherwise known as H-Money, Waldo, Delivery, and Hazzle Dazzle, are here to clear the streets of drowsiness.
